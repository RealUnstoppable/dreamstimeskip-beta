Dreams TimeSkip: Patch Notes

"The Legends of Time Unveiled: Rise of the Exiles"

Centuries have passed since the first exiles set foot in the mysterious lands of time. They, the pioneers who forged the NeoCube, a sacred artifact capable of bending reality itself, unknowingly unleashed a force that would shape the fate of kingdoms.

With its discovery, an enigmatic figure emerged, wielding the NeoCube's power to control these lands and dictate the rise and fall of mighty realms. Yet, amidst this dominion, a glimmer of resistance remains.

Now, the remnants of noble bloodlines have splintered, each vying to forge their own kingdoms. As chaos consumes the realm, the lone Minister watches from the Throne Room. A shadow looms from the underworld, an Overlord ascends. It falls to you, brave Exile, to gather your loyal subjects and claim your birthright.

New Content & Tutorials

Gilded Items

How to craft gilded gear:

  1. Burn Gilded Blackstone in a furnace or blast furnace.
  2. Make Gilded Netherite Ingots and combine with diamond equipment OR directly apply to netherite equipment.

Bonus: 1 or more pieces of armor makes piglins ignore you. A full set gives you +4 extra health.

  • Gilded Netherite Helmet: 543 durability (vs 407 standard)
  • Gilded Netherite Chestplate: 789 durability (vs 592 standard)
  • Gilded Netherite Sword: 9 damage, 2539 durability

New Weapon: Maces

Bash Attack (Right Click / Hold): Deals massive durability damage to armor.

  • Gilded Netherite Mace: 50 durability damage
  • Netherite Mace: 42 durability damage
  • Diamond Mace: 35 durability damage

World Generation: Biomania

Travel new biomes and explore the landscape. Includes new mobs, plants, flowers, trees, and building blocks.

  • Cherry Blossom Grove: Full of pink petals and Cherry trees.
  • Shattered Taiga: A chaotic, rugged landscape.
  • Dunes: Vast sandy expanses with new vegetation.
  • Rainforest: Dense, lush, and full of life.
  • Shrubland & Pumpkin Patches: New variations to explore.

Magic Addon

We have added 30 new wands and 10 magic classes! Each player can have at most 2 magic classes at a time. There is a leveling system to unlock new magic to prevent players from becoming too OP immediately.

Magic Types: Wind, Water, Poison, Necromancy, Light, Ice, Grass, Fire, Electric, Death.

Craft the Magic Book to get started.

Furniture & Decor

Decorate your medieval houses with 3D models including:

  • Decorative Sticks & Beams
  • Bookshelves with variants
  • Crates, Barrels, and Baskets (Apple, Carrot, Fish, etc.)
  • Tables, Chairs (Functional!), Stools, and Supports
  • Cupboards and Desks

Admin Command: /function medieval_blocks

Economy & Systems

New Tax System

Taxes are based on monthly shop income:

  • 0 - 100 Gold: 5%
  • 100 - 500 Gold: 10%
  • 500 - 1000 Gold: 15%
  • 1000+ Gold: 20%

Currency Conversion

  • 1 Iron Ingot = 0.2 Gold
  • 1 Gold Bar = 20 Gold
  • 1 Diamond = 50 Gold
  • 1 Netherite Ingot = 100 Gold
